Learning is fundamentally difficult and it is by design. Learning anything worthwhile should be difficult in my view; if the struggle is not there; it is very likely that learning is more of rote or relying on some other existing structure inside brain. 
If someone is learning to play a video game and they have had not any serious experience with that kind of game, they will find the process quite difficult in the beginning because that person is being forced to look at a new and unfamiliar kind of system. Our brains seriously hate when things don't make sense directly; there might be evolutionary links to that.
