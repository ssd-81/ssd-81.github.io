## what is this really? why India Stack? 
I have been hearing about India Stack from quite some time, but I did not give it a thought deeply. Recently I have come across some startups which are essentially built on the foundation of India Stack, which sparked great interest in me to actually understand those problems and then take it up from there. 

What is India Stack? It is a loaded term which encompasses set of technologies and frameworks which involve setting up the infrastrcuture of India. This includes tech like UPI, e-KYC and DigiLocker. None of these technologies are owned by a single authority. Most of the India stack is built on top of open source; it uses open standards, but it is intended for India (fine tuned in a way). But, the overall setup can be easily transferred to other countries. 

What problem does it solve? The system is essentially designed to solve for a large surface area. I am not sure what precisely it solves, but it is likely a project that targets three facets: Identity (giving representation to people from every background in a structured manner), payments (reducing resistance for money transfer between parties) and management (data security, privacy and who gets to know what data in the process). If we look at this from larger perspectives, the problem it solves is likely systems level and infrastructural. The outcomes might not be clearly visible, but it is foundational groundwork on which other organizations and companies operate on. 
I have a very surface level understanding for now. I will try to refine it further by actually touching the problem it tries to solve.
