Writing seems like a very vague idea if we think about it how it is perceived amongst the masses. There are two common views; either it is about creating something that is too original or it is as mundane it can get. The idea of writing as a skill sits in between that spectrum. I view writing as somewhat differently; it is way to construct thought which eventually turns to plans or system, that we end up following. Good writing does not need to be accurate or filled with heavy words, but rather conveying an idea properly. 
I, myself am pretty bad at it; but just like everything else; writing is a skill. It goes way deeper than most of the skills; because in some way or the other; writing is required. To simplify: **it is the meta skill**, I had to say it.
